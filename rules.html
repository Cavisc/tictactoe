TOE

The board is a three-row-by-three-column matrix.

Two players each choose a tag, usually a circle (O) and an X (X).

Players take turns, one mark at a time, in an empty space.

The object is to get three circles or three crosses in a row, either horizontally, vertically or diagonally, and at the same time, where possible, prevent the opponent from winning on the next turn.

If both players always play at their best, the game will always end in a tie.
